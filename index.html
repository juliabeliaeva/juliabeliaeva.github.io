Hi, I'm Julia. I am a software developer living in Saint-Petersburg, Russia. For the past 11+ years I've been working at JetBrains, a company that make the best tools for software development. I'm currently a member of Version Control team in IntelliJ IDEA, where I work on tools to view repository log and file history for Git and Mercurial version control systems.

Apart from coding, I love rock climbing. As a member of Polytechnic Mountaineering Club, I climbed in Caucasus Mountains, in the French, Italian and German Apls, in Khibiny, and in Tian Shan. At the club I used to help instructors training newcomers working with ropes, in belaying techniques, building anchors (I've written a short instructional manual in Russian about that), etc. In the recent years my focus shifted to bouldering. I also used to do running, my best result was finishing Saint-Petersburg White Nights marathon in 4:23.

My other hobbies include photography, reading popular-science books about physics and riding a kick-scooter.
